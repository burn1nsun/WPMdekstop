Calculate your WPM from anywhere

WPMdesktop listens to your keystrokes on the system level, which means it can calculate your WPM from anywhere.

Live counting

WPMdesktop allows you to see your WPM in realtime with an optional and effective window over your taskbar!

Idle pausing

A practical feature for counting WPM over a long period is automatically pausing during idle time, for example whilst writing an essay to collect your thoughts.
